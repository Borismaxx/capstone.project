About Me

Currently pursuing a Higher National Diploma (HND 1) in Civil Engineering at Federal Polytechnic Nekede with a National Diploma from Federal Polytechnic Owerri,ialso spends time on set as an actor.

Whether he is analyzing the latest Nigerian music streams, designing furniture layouts, or managing international projects, he brings a dedicated and dynamic energy to a diverse range of interests.

Beyond traditional civil engineering, he is actively expanding his technical skillset into software and full-stack web development.
